Fantastic Wild Animals for kids!

Explore the amazing world of wild animals! Learn fun facts about their
size, weight, what they eat, and how long they live. From lions and bears
to sharks, discover fascinating creatures in a way that's easy and fun for kids.

Animals

Name Description Characteristics Image
Bear Bears are large, stocky, shaggy-haired mammals in the family Ursidae, with large bodies, short tails, long snouts, and powerful, non-retractable claws on plantigrade paws.
  • Weight: 27 - 726 kg (depends on species)
  • Size: 1.2 - 2.4 m (depends on species)
  • Main food sources: The diet of bears varies depending on the species: some eat salmon, others bamboo, and some eat seals
  • Life expectancy: 25 years in the wild, up to 50 years in captivity (depends on species)
Shark Sharks are fast-swimming fishes that have a skeleton made of cartilage instead of bone. (Cartilage is an elastic tissue that is created before bone begins to form.) Sharks are among the oldest animals on Earth.
  • Weight: 20kg - 1,000+ kg (varies by species)
  • Size: 0.5m - 12m (dwarf lanternshark ~20cm, whale shark ~12m)
  • Main food sources: Fish, squid, crustaceans, marine mammals (depends on species)
  • Life expectancy: 20 - 70+ years (depends on species)
Lion Lions are big cats that live in Africa. They live in groups called prides. Male lions have a big mane, and they are very strong hunters.
  • Weight: 120 - 250 kg (depends on species and sex; males are heavier)
  • Size: 1.4 - 2.5 m (body length, not including tail)
  • Main food sources: Zebras, antelopes, buffalo, and other animals (depends on what's available)
  • Life expectancy: 10 - 14 years in the wild, up to 20 years in captivity
